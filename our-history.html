OUR HISTORY

For 35 years, we have been imagining and designing tailor-made, customizable housing, in perfect harmony with their environment and individual needs.

Our story is born from a societal objective: to offer modern, high-quality, efficient and affordable housing to accommodate young people, single-parent families, seniors and single people.

To meet this objective, we have specialized in the assembly of small operations, from 4 batches, up to 40 batches. In the form of adjoining houses, collective dwellings up to R + 2, from 4 to 12 dwellings. By fitting out several buildings on a plot, in mixed projects: land + collective.

True architectural reflection, all of our real estate programs reaffirm quality of life as an essential pillar of modern housing and “better living together”.

WE DESIGN LIVING SPACES

BOURGET HABITAT offers new , quality and accessible housing that is brighter, more spacious, customizable and eco-responsible. (Re) taste the pleasure of large private and common outdoor spaces where life is good.

Our core business? Intermediate accommodation, halfway between the house and the collective building to offer you a new way of living. We are developing this know-how to offer you new affordable, comfortable and more human-sized residences than ever.
